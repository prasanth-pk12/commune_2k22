About Commune Program

i. Commune 2022 Prof. M. Sarlinraj presented the Commune 2022 Proposal to the faculty members. The following suggestions were made by the faculty members: 1. Events are to be conducted before the stage programme. 2. On the final day a workshop can be conducted for half a day. Lunch can be provided to all the students and faculty. In the afternoon the star of commune event can be conducted. After that the winner’s presentations can be exhibited. Then finally the stage function can be held. 3. On the day of conducting events, Quiz can be organized based on the event through Moodle platform in order to make the students to learn about the topics involved in the event. 4. Introducing two rolling trophies for the best participation among the 1st years of both shifts and among the 2nd years of both shifts in order to increase the participation of the students. The calculation of total points for winning the trophy is as follows: Each participants – 1 point (No. of participants in the class / Total no. of students in the class) 5th Place – 1 point 4th Place – 2 points 3rd Place – 3 points 2nd Place – 4 points 1st Place – 5 points 5. The conduction of each event can be considered as an association activity.
